About Madeline

Madeline Cochran is a Minneapolis Based UX Designer, Rearcher and artist. She recieved her BA from the University of Saint John’s, College of Saint Benedict’s in 2017 and an MFA from Duncan of Jordanstone College of Art and Design in 2018. She has since worked in non-profits and retail is currently the Gift Shop Manager at Franconia Sculpture Park. Skills: Adobe Creative Cloud, Excel Spreadsheets, Salesforce, Square, Letterpresss printing, Marketing
