Case Study: Can do Canines - Website Redesign

Problems:

Can do Canines does not allow you to choose your own dog, it is always assigned to the individual. User’s wanted to know more about their forever friend. User’s felt that the content and information was very thorough and specialized, they felt like any questions they had could be answered on the site.

Process:

First conducted user testing interviews and redesigned site according to user stuggles.

My Design Solutions:

Aided in organizing text and condensing information on text heavy site.
